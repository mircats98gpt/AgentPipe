--- 
title: Orange Peel Soup
description: |
  This goes great with broth of molasses, a fresh breath from the roman baths!
---

## Ingredients

| Name | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Orange Peels | 6 Cups |
| Bathwater | 3 Gal |

## Backstory

Long ago i was in a bath.
Not just any bath.
A bathtub with capybara,
some call them "carpincho"
but that sounds too much like they bite,
when really what they love is oranges.

Oranges they brought,
and brought by the plenty.
All around me oranges,
wearing as a hat,
squeezing against the eye,
and oh so juicy,
sweet oranges.

And we bathed and talked and passed the time.
Not noticing that our oranges had begun to flavor the bath around us.
And we drank and drank,
the most effluvescent soup,
just our essences and the oranges.

Perfect for an afternoon pick me up, 
warms the spirit on a fall day.

How do you get festive with your orange peel soup?

## Steps

- Heat a hot bath
- Express zest of orange peels before entering
- Enter bath, carrying oranges
- Optional: Invite a band of capybara for additional seasoning
- Boil 45m
- Enjoy
